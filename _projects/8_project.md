<h2>Overview</h2>

<p>
The <strong>TeleAqua</strong> series is a family of cross-medium aerial–aquatic robots
designed for portability, rapid deployment, and high-performance operation across
air and underwater environments.
</p>

<ul>
<li>
<strong>TeleAqua-Bee</strong> (&lt; 1 kg) fits easily into a backpack while delivering
<strong>10 minutes</strong> of aerial endurance and <strong>30 minutes</strong> underwater.
It features automatic surface self-righting and stable hovering for safe,
portable operation.
</li>

<li>
<strong>TeleAqua-H8</strong> emphasizes rapid response and heavy payload capacity,
supporting up to <strong>5 kg</strong>. It provides <strong>15 minutes</strong> of aerial
flight and up to <strong>1 hour</strong> underwater with a <strong>10 m</strong> dive depth,
enhanced by stereo perception and quick-release modular payloads.
</li>

<li>
<strong>TeleAqua-HZ</strong> features foldable arms and a streamlined morphing design,
transforming from quadrotor to underwater vehicle. It reaches speeds over
<strong>4 knots</strong> (&gt; 2 m/s) while carrying <strong>700 g</strong>.
</li>

<li>
<strong>TeleAqua-Edu</strong> provides an accessible platform for education, rapid
prototyping, and cross-medium robotics research.
</li>
</ul>
